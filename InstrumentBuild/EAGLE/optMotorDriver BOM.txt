Build Notes:

Stepper motor mounts to A and B coil ports. Connect light sources to 'ChanX' output terminal block.  
Thorlabs LED T cubes connect through BNC pigtail cable. 
Connecting camera to trigger terminal is optional.  
Power through wall wart supply with 5.5 mm x 2.1 mm connector, 12v, 0.5 A output.

Button board lets you switch on/off channels, move motor outside the enclosure.
